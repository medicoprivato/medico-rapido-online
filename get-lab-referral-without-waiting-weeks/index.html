How to get a lab test referral without waiting for weeks

Medico Subito Editorial Team

Needing a routine blood test or lab exam should be simple, but in practice it often means waiting weeks just to get an appointment with a doctor who can write the referral in the first place.

A faster path for non-urgent requests

For lab test referrals that don't require an in-person examination first, such as routine blood work, thyroid panels, vitamin levels, or follow-up testing for an already-known condition, a written medical consultation can produce a signed referral within 24 hours.

How the process works

You describe what you need tested and why, ideally including any relevant medical history or previous results. The doctor reviews the request and, if appropriate, issues a written and signed referral that you can take to any private lab.

When an in-person visit is still necessary

If your situation suggests a new symptom that requires a physical examination, or if the requested tests fall outside what can be safely assessed in writing, a responsible doctor will tell you so rather than issuing a referral regardless. This service is designed for routine, non-urgent testing needs, not for new complex diagnostic workups.

Why this matters for regular monitoring

For people managing chronic conditions who need periodic blood work, a fast and reliable way to get a referral without a long wait can make a real difference in staying on top of routine monitoring, rather than delaying tests simply because booking an appointment takes too long.

For emergencies always call 118 or go to the nearest Emergency Room. This service is for non-urgent needs.
