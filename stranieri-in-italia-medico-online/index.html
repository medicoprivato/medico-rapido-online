🇬🇧 Living in Italy without a doctor?

If you live in Italy and are not registered with the National Health Service (SSN), getting medical help can be complicated and expensive. Medico Subito offers an affordable alternative: write to our doctor anytime, get a written response within 24 hours.

Private prescriptions valid in any Italian pharmacy
Blood tests and exams request for any private lab
Medical certificates for personal use
Written medical consultations in Italian or English
Phone teleconsultation on request, no appointment, no waiting room

How it works / Come funziona

1. Write your request anytime / Scrivi la tua richiesta quando vuoi
2. The doctor reads and evaluates it / Il medico la legge e valuta
3. Signed PDF response within 24 hours / Risposta firmata in PDF entro 24 ore

Important / Importante

For emergencies, call 118 (Italian emergency number) or go to the nearest Pronto Soccorso (emergency room).

Per le emergenze, chiama il 118 o recati al Pronto Soccorso più vicino.

Available worldwide / Disponibile in tutto il mondo

Even if you live outside Italy, you can use Medico Subito for:

Written medical consultations — get a professional written opinion on your symptoms, diagnosis or treatment
Second opinion — send your medical records or test results and receive an independent evaluation
Interpretation of medical reports — TAC, MRI, blood tests, ultrasound: we explain what they mean
Guidance on your care path — what specialist to see, what exams to request, what questions to ask your local doctor

Note: private prescriptions are valid only in Italian pharmacies. For patients outside Italy, we provide written medical consultations and second opinions that you can share with your local doctor.
